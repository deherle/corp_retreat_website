Modern Luxury on the Lake

Lake Cowichan Corporate Retreat Rental

Welcome to our Lake Cowichan Home! Built in 2021, our lake house offers 2700 sq. ft. of modern luxury. We're offering the full house for corporate day use, which includes team offsites, strategy sessions, teambuilding and more.

Lake Haven offers true indoor-outdoor living with over 1500 sq. ft. of composite decks, 7-person hot tub, 12' conference table, 4 TVs, chef's kitchen, games room, (2) 5kW outdoor heaters, fire pit and BBQ. Come any season; our corporate retreat offers experiences all year round.

Interested in renting the lower suite for a personal stay? See our sister website for information.

We're conveniently located 1-hour from Victoria or Nanaimo.

Property Highlights

Fabulous Views

The property and (3!) decks offer stunning views of Lake Cowichan and Bald Mountain. Find inspiration without leaving the retreat.

Beach and Park Access

Need some downtime between sessions? Looking for inspiration? We're steps from Bald Mountain, hiking trails and the beach at Stoker Park.

Hiking and more

Hiking and biking trails abound just steps from our property. Access Bald Mountain and Bald Mountain Park 200m away.

Quiet Neighborhood

Woodland Shores is a serene, placid environment to relax and enjoy lake and mountain views. Herds of Elk regularly peruse the area.
